# Product description

ShakeUp is a mobile application developed to help users look up nutritional values and diet compatibility of drinks, shakes, and beverages from licensed sources. It takes the mental load of calculations off those interested in food tracking and provides a clean, clear summary of nutritional composition. Aside from a summary, the user can see a table that provides an extensive overview of minerals, vitamins, and macronutrients, along with their respective daily values.

Through the app, users can create their own drinks, organize them into collections, create random “surprise” shakes, and easily share their made concoctions with friends. Additionally, users can look up the most common ingredients and their full nutritional data in its dedicated offline-accessible library. It includes information such as diet compatibility and personal recommendations based on user preferences and needs. 

The app stands out thanks to well-suited, energetic animations, cartoonish sound effects, and a simple, modern, minimalist, sleek, and vibrant design. It facilitates frictionless execution of beverage-building tasks with a reduced cognitive load. Suited for an on-the-go lifestyle of people who place a high value on their nourishment. The color scheme, font size, sound effects, theme, and animation properties can be tailored to user preferences in settings.

Architecturally, the app is lightweight, fetching database data on the fly and providing local caching so users can access saved foods offline. If an item is missing from the database, users can manually log custom ingredients. The app is open-source. It doesn’t store or process user-related information anywhere but locally. It’s completely ad-free and free of charge to provide a seamless experience.

To guarantee that ShakeUp stays free, collaborative, and accessible to everyone, the application is licensed under the GNU General Public License v3 (GPLv3). The app remains free to run, study, share, and modify. This licensing aligns with our "local-first" data privacy model, giving users absolute transparency over how their nutritional data is handled.
